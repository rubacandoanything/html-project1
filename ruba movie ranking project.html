BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO RUBA

My top three movies of all time

INCEPTION

Watching this movie was like going on a mind-blowing mind bender, right from the start.
The overarching concept of implanting a thought or idea into someone mind and having things transgress from there was very intriguing.
I like the exploration of dreams and the different levels of dreams and questioning and bending of reality.

JOHN WICK FRANCHISE

A must watch action fick with abundance of assassinations.

THE GRAND BUDAPEST HOTEL

It is one of the most beautiful piece of cinema created.
